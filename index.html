Gajar ka Halwa

ingredients

  • carrot - 700 gns
  • milk mava - 200 gm
  • sugar - 300 gm
  • Dry fruits - 1 small bowl
    (almond pistachio,cashew)
  • Cardamon - 1/2 tbsp
  • Claeified butter (ghee) 1 tbsp

procedure

  1. Grale the carrots
  2. Take a pan and add grated carrot.
    Let it on the flame until the water in the carrot evaporates.
    Keep stirring frome time to time.
  3. Now add sugar . Mix it well and keep stirring.
    Cook till the sugar is completely dissolve
    into a syrup . Now keep stirring till the final
    water content in the mixture evaporates
  4. Add clarified butter and cardamom powder
    and stir the mixture
  5. Now add milk mava and mash it
    completely with the ghee mixture in another pan.
    And roast it for a bit. then add dry fruits and stir.
  6. Now add the sweetened carrot mix to this
    mixture of milk mava. stir and mix everything completely.
    Take care it doesn't burn out at the bottom.
  7. Garnish it with dry fruits and serve hot
